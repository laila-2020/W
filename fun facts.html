Discover amazing, surprising, and funny facts about animals and nature that will make you see wildlife in a whole new way!

Animal fun facts

Elephants are the only animals that can’t jump. A group of flamingos is called a “flamboyance.” Tigers have striped skin, not just striped fur. Octopuses have three hearts. Sloths can hold their breath longer than dolphins. Dolphins have names for each other. Kangaroos can’t walk backward.

Nature fun facts

Bamboo can grow up to 35 inches in a single day. Rainforests produce over 20% of the world’s oxygen. Some trees can live for thousands of years. Volcanoes can create new islands. Coral reefs are home to 25% of all marine life.

Weired and interesting facts

Sea otters hold hands while sleeping to stay together. A shrimp’s heart is in its head. Penguins propose to their mates with pebbles. Frogs can freeze completely and come back to life. Crows can recognize human faces and remember them for years.
