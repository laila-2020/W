Zebra

Zebras are African herbivores known for their
unique black-and-white stripes. They live in herds,
feeding mainly on grasses, and their
stripes help with camouflage and recognition.

Cats

Cats are small carnivorous mammals found worldwide, known for their
agility, sharp senses, and playful nature.
They are often kept as pets but also live in the wild as skilled hunters.

Dogs

Dogs are loyal and intelligent mammals that have lived alongside humans for thousands of years. Found worldwide, they are kept as pets, protectors, and working animals. With sharp senses and friendly nature, dogs are valued for companionship and their many helpful roles in human life.
.

Horse

Horses are strong herbivorous mammals known for their speed and endurance.
They have been close to humans for thousands of years,
used for travel, farming, and sport. With their graceful
movements and loyal nature, horses remain
one of the most admired animals worldwide.
.

Elephant

Elephants are the largest land mammals, recognized by their trunks and tusks. Living in Africa and Asia, they are gentle giants that eat plants and play a key role in shaping their habitats.

Lion

Lions are powerful carnivorous mammals often called the "king of the jungle." They live in prides, mainly in African grasslands, and are known for their strength, courage, and loud roars.


Bear

Bears are large mammals found in forests, mountains, and icy regions. They are strong, adaptable, and feed on a mix of plants, fish, and small animals.

Tiger

Tigers are the largest wild cats, known for their striking orange coats with black stripes. Native to Asia, they are solitary hunters that rely on stealth and strength to catch prey.

Giraffe

Giraffes are tall herbivores famous for their long necks and spotted coats. Found in African savannas, they feed on leaves high in trees and move gracefully despite their height..

Dolphin

.Dolphins are intelligent marine mammals known for their playful behavior and communication skills. Living in oceans worldwide, they are social animals that often travel in groups called pods.

Crocodile

Crocodiles are large reptiles that live in rivers, lakes, and wetlands. With powerful jaws and stealthy movements, they are among the oldest surviving predators on Earth..

Wolf

Wolves are social carnivores that live in packs, known for their teamwork and howls. They roam forests, tundras, and grasslands, playing an important role as top predators.
